Mother Monkey

Re-live the riffs. Re-live the 90s. Your youth, louder than ever.

Mother Monkey were created, when after a hiatus with many other bands, four teenage pals regrouped to find their love of 1990s rock with one thing in mind; recreating that sound and playing it vibrantly for the masses.

Mother Monkey are available to book for pubs, clubs or other events in and around Sussex and Surrey (and sometimes further afield). We can provide our own PA system and lighting.

Book us to provide a loud, rocky, grungey evening with lairy songs by Red Hot Chili Peppers, Green Day, Foo Fighters and more!
